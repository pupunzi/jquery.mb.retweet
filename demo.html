mb.retweet.demo

The mb.retweet let your visitors to easly retweet your page with a predefined comment, numbers of retweet and the short URL of your page (using the Bit.ly API).
To get a user and a key for Bit.ly go to http://bit.ly/account/your_api_key.
If you don't whant to get an account on Bit.ly to short your URLs, the component will use the original URL instead, but you'll not have retweetted number.
